Chapter 49

In this very 1961, Douglas Doragaru started an orphanage for orphan children in Jangama, Warangal district.
Along with this, in just a few years, many Children’s Homes were established all over India because David garu stood with Douglas garu.
Along with them, it developed into an organization that provided financial help to thousands of gospel workers.
Before entering the year 1962, the Watchnight Service took place very grandly. Proclaiming the gospel through the mike until morning, they reached home, and the New Year worship continued until 4 o’clock very blessedly.
In the same month, meetings took place in Kondaguturu from the 8th to 10th.
8 people received baptism. From the 15th to 18th, meetings took place in Gokavaram.
Dr. Douglas garu and Dr. Peptas Thomas garu delivered the Word; David Ayyagaru translated.
24 people received baptism. After that, going with them to Srikakulam, they made arrangements to buy a place for the hostel.
From there, via Kazipet, they reached Jangama, visited the hostel started there, and with Douglas garu went by plane to Bangalore; from there they reached Cochin.
After seeing P.J. Thomas garu’s work, from there they went to the Kariyampalavu Convention, served along with Douglas garu, returned, traveled on the GT Express on the 28th and 29th, went to Delhi, welcomed Apostle P.M. Samuel garu from abroad, came with him by plane to Hyderabad, from there reached Vijayawada, concluded the welcome meeting, and David Ayyagaru went for the arrangements of the Eluru Mahasabha.
From February 14 to 18, those meetings took place very grandly.
From March 1 to 4, Rajahmundry meetings took place. In them, Apo. P.M. Samuel garu, Paranjyoti garu, T.Y. Shastri garu, E.S. Paul garu delivered the Word.
26 people received baptisms.

On the 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th they announced fasting prayers and prayed.
David Ayyagaru submitted petitions to the authorities and the Collector.
As a reward for the prayers, a miracle happened. By the time the officer came, it became interval time for the National School; all the children were playing in that place; seeing the church right next to it, the Collector canceled the permission and left.
The church praised God with great joy.
On the evening of November 12, 1962, pains came again to Ammagaru for delivery.
Leaving Ammagaru entrusted to the believers at home, he went to the servants’ meeting taking place in Rajanagaram and returned at night.
On the 13th at early dawn, Ammagaru gave birth.
The born girl resembled her mother, and thinking that in the coming days she should be a child with the dedication to serve like her mother, they named her Leela Rachel.
In 1963, the Watchnight Service took place very blessedly.
From February 27 to March 1, 1964, meetings took place in Rajahmundry.
In these meetings, Dr. Douglas delivered the divine Word solemnly.
Along with him, E.S. Paul garu and T.N. Shastri garu also gave divine messages.
In this way, in this year up to the month of June, they conducted 22 gospel meetings in the area.
They gave baptisms to hundreds of people.
For the Kovvur branch church temple construction, on the 13th of the 4th month 1964, they bought the place and registered it.
On October 19 evening, Ammagaru gave birth and bore a son; since he was very cute, they named him Sweet Babu.
When many troubles came organizationally and institutionally, patiently he visited many churches and reconciled many people.
David Ayyagaru’s service increased, time became less.
In that way, for the expansion of God’s kingdom, day and night he ran in service.
Sometimes without travel facilities, sometimes on bicycle, on motorcycle, he traveled hundreds of kilometers and strengthened the churches.
In 1965, on May 9, Leelamma garu slept in the Lord.
